Zhe Meng

Lecturer

Xi'an University of Posts and Telecommunications

Biography

Zhe Meng received the B.S. and Ph.D. degrees from Xidian University, Xi’an, China, in 2014 and 2020, respectively. He is currently a Lecturer with the School of Telecommunication and Information Engineering, Xi’an University of Posts and Telecommunications. His research interests include deep learning and hyperspectral image classification.
